Sacred Rectangle (Personal Project)

Sacred Rectangle is a "Twin Stick Shooter" mixed with Typing game, it's main focus is using your keyboard as much as possible. This game is born from my hobby of building custom mechanical keyboard.

The controls are W,S,A,D for directional movement with the optional I,J,K,L for look direction. The reason I chose this control is so that players will only need to keep their hands at their keyboard to play.


The reloading mechanic will be one of the main feature of the game, you need to reload your letters, so that the letters you're gonna be firing will be accurate or letter you want. this can be optional too since if you don't have letters in your clip it will just fire random letters


This is where the reloading mechanic will come in handy, Enemies will come in two types of health, their normal health and their word health, you can shoot them with random letters that are not corresponding with their health but, the player will do minuscule damage, unlike firing the right letters, where firing the right letter will do most of the damage.


It will feature a simple AI state machine, the reason for this is the gameplay, I plan for this to be a adventure game, so I think the enemies needed to have a brain a little. Also planning to do complex state machine for the bosses but I'm not anywhere near that task.


It will also feature Keyboard Customization, you can change the keyset of your keyboard for the looks but my main idea for this is, the players can also change their keyboard case and switches, this customization will affect the gameplay.


Snapshot

Snapshot is a isometric puzzle platformer game, that features a story line. You're playing as the intern and you need to restore the memory of the camera, memory that has been lost by the owner, using the camera's function, the Shutter Speed, Aperture and the ISO you need to complete levels and restore the memories.

Snapshot is my teams CAPSTN game, it won the best CAPSTN at the time, I was responsible for the camera function mechanics and its effects, Save Management, Objectives and the games UI.
